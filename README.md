## FAQ

**Q: Will a VPN lower my ping?**  
A: Sometimes, if your ISP route is messy. Most of the time it adds a small overhead. Use it for stability/privacy first, then test regions.

**Q: What server should I pick for gaming?**  
A: Start with the closest **Global Servers** location to your game’s region. Then test 2–3 nearby nodes and keep the best.

**Q: Does the Kill Switch matter?**  
A: Yes. If the tunnel drops mid-match, the **Kill Switch** prevents your traffic from leaking outside the VPN.

**Q: Is this focused on privacy or gaming performance?**  
A: Both. The baseline is **privacy and security focus** (AES-256, No-Logs policy). The review notes prioritize **high speed** and **stable connection** behavior for games.
